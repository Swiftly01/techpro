Tech-A-Child

Tech-A-Child is our social responsibility initiative dedicated to empowering children and young graduates with essential tech skills at no cost. We believe that equipping these young minds with in-demand tech skills will foster responsible and capable future citizens. We invite enthusiastic individuals to sponsor a Tech-A-Child, either from your network or from our waiting list. If you’re interested in making a difference, reach out to us to sponsor a child and contribute to their bright future.
